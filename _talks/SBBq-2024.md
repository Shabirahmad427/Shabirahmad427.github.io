**SBBQ 2024** is the annual meeting of the **Sociedade Brasileira de Bioquímica e Biologia Molecular (SBBQ)**, one of the main scientific conferences in Brazil dedicated to biochemistry, molecular biology, biophysics, and computational approaches applied to biological systems.

**Presenter:** Shabir Ahmad  

**Abstract:**  
This presentation discussed recent advances in molecular dynamics simulations and computational modeling of biomolecular systems, highlighting mechanistic insights into structure–function relationships and enzymatic processes. The work was presented during **SBBQ 2024**, contributing to interdisciplinary discussions at the interface of biochemistry, molecular biology, and computational science.
