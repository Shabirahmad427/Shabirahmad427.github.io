**SBBf 2023** is the annual congress of the **Sociedade Brasileira de Biofísica (SBBf)**, bringing together researchers in biophysics, structural biology, molecular simulations, and computational biophysics.

**Presenter:** Shabir Ahmad  

**Abstract:**  
This presentation reported recent results in molecular dynamics and computational biophysics, with emphasis on structure–function relationships in biological macromolecules and advanced simulation methodologies. The contribution was presented during **SBBf 2023**, fostering scientific exchange within the Brazilian and international biophysics community.
