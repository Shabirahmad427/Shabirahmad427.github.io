**CCES-CEPID 2024** is an annual scientific event organized by the **Center for Computing in Engineering & Sciences (CCES)**, a FAPESP CEPID research center hosted at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP). The meeting brings together researchers working in computational modeling, high-performance computing, molecular simulations, and data-driven science.

**Speaker:** Shabir Ahmad  

**Abstract:**  
This talk presented recent advances in computational enzymology and molecular dynamics simulations, highlighting how high-performance computing and advanced sampling techniques can be used to investigate structure–function relationships in complex biological systems.
