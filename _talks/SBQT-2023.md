**SBQT 2023** is the annual meeting of the **Simpósio Brasileiro de Química Teórica**, a major Brazilian conference dedicated to theoretical and computational chemistry, including molecular simulations, electronic structure theory, statistical mechanics, and multiscale modeling.

**Presenter:** Shabir Ahmad  

**Abstract:**  
This presentation reported recent results in theoretical and computational chemistry, with emphasis on molecular dynamics simulations and their application to understanding structure–function relationships in complex chemical and biological systems. The contribution was presented during **SBQT 2023**, fostering scientific discussion and collaboration within the Brazilian theoretical chemistry community.
